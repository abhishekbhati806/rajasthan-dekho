Sample Itinerary

Day-Wise Journey

Day 1

Jaipur — The Pink City

Begin at the majestic Amer Fort, stroll through City Palace's grand courtyards, marvel at Hawa Mahal's 953 windows, and end your evening at the vibrant Chokhi Dhani.

Amer Fort City Palace Hawa Mahal Chokhi Dhani
Day 2

Jodhpur — The Blue City

Explore the towering Mehrangarh Fort, visit the serene Jaswant Thada, wander the spice-filled Clock Tower market, and lose yourself in the indigo-painted lanes of the old city.

Mehrangarh Fort Jaswant Thada Clock Tower Blue City Walk
Day 3

Jaisalmer — The Golden City

Walk the living Jaisalmer Fort's ancient sandstone streets, discover the intricate carvings of Patwon Ki Haveli, relax at Gadisar Lake, and end with a magical desert safari under the stars.

Jaisalmer Fort Patwon Ki Haveli Gadisar Lake Desert Safari
Day 4

Udaipur — The City of Lakes

Admire the grandeur of City Palace overlooking shimmering Lake Pichola, take a serene boat ride, seek blessings at Jagdish Temple, and witness the folk dances of Bagore Ki Haveli.

City Palace Lake Pichola Jagdish Temple Bagore Ki Haveli
Heritage Destinations

Iconic Landmarks

Jaipur

Hawa Mahal

9am – 5pm ₹50

Palace of Winds with 953 lattice windows — built in 1799 for royal ladies to observe street life.

Jodhpur

Mehrangarh Fort

9am – 5pm ₹100

One of India's largest forts, perched 400ft above Jodhpur — founded by Rao Jodha in 1459.

Jaisalmer

Jaisalmer Fort

Sunrise – Sunset Free

A UNESCO-listed golden living fort built in 1156 — merchants, temples, and havelis inside.

Udaipur

City Palace Udaipur

9:30am – 5:30pm ₹150

Eleven palaces complexed overlooking Lake Pichola — started by Maharana Udai Singh II in 1553.
